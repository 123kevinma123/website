ABOUT

INTRODUCTION


   Hey! I'm Kevin Ma, the creator of this website. I'm currently a sophmore in Computer Science attending Purdue University. In my free time, I enjoy chilling with friends, playing the violin, or coding side projects such as this website.

THIS WEBSITE


   The first iteration of this website was just a typical run of the mill website. It lacked something special and unique. Frankly, I didn't feel the spark to actively try and improve on it. Around Christmas, I received a video game called UNDERTALE, which had a pixelated esque artstyle. This drew my attention, and I became rather fixated on the idea of a retro-inspired website. Thus, this website became a passion project for me.

FUTURE PLANS


   Over the summer, I plan on creating and adding some retro video games under the "Play Games" section to both sharpen my programming skills and add a fun element to my website. There are still many formatting issues and feature I want to address, but the website is coming along nicely so far!
